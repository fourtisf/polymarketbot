04 — Construction

grid · proportions · safe area
Conceptrationale

The mark is an “A” built from two ascending strokes — a candlestick wick rising, a portfolio curve trending up. The crossbar is broken into a chart tick, and a single node sits at the apex: the AGI dot, the intelligence inside the capital.

  • Monoline — same stroke weight at every size
  • Geometric base — circle & isosceles triangle on a 32×32 grid
  • Right leg pitched 2° steeper to suggest forward momentum
  • Apex node sized to read at 12px favicon
