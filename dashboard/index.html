Strategy

Edge, not luck.

The bot trades only when a measurable price signal appears in the last seconds of a 5-minute window — and refuses to trade when conditions don't justify risk. Here's the loop, every 5 minutes:

Capture the price-to-beat

At each 5-minute boundary, lock in the Chainlink-anchored opening price that the binary market resolves against.

Score the signal

Combine BTC delta vs. price-to-beat, Binance volume regime, order book depth, and trend persistence into a 0-100 confidence score.

Enter only on edge

Only when score ≥ 65 and Δ ≥ 0.08% in the T-30s → T-8s window does the bot place a sized limit order. Otherwise, skip.

Resolve & log

At window close, on-chain settlement records the outcome. P&L, score, and decision log are public on this page.
